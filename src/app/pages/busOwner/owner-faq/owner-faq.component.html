Frequently asked questions

How do I register my bus fleet with BusNGo?

To register your bus fleet with BusNGo, click on the "SIGN IN / SIGN UP" link at the top right corner of the registration form, fill in your mobile number, and follow the prompts to complete your registration.

What documents are required for registration?

You will need to provide a valid government-issued ID, proof of ownership for your buses, and other relevant documents that verify the legal operation of your fleet.

How does BusNGo help increase my ticket sales?

BusNGo connects your bus fleet with millions of travelers through its extensive e-ticketing platform, ensuring higher visibility and more bookings for your buses.

What support does BusNGo offer to bus operators?

BusNGo provides 24/7 support to all registered bus operators. Our dedicated team assists with technical issues, booking management, and any other queries you may have.

Feel free to contact us for any assistance you need.

Are there any fees associated with registering my fleet?

Yes, there are registration fees and transaction fees associated with listing your buses on BusNGo. Please contact our support team for detailed information on the fee structure.

How do I manage my bus schedules and fares?

Once registered, you can log in to the BusNGo operator portal, where you can easily manage your bus schedules, set fares, and monitor bookings in real-time.

What happens if a bus gets delayed or cancelled?

In the event of delays or cancellations, you can update the status through the operator portal. BusNGo will notify affected passengers and offer rebooking or refund options as per your policy.

How does BusNGo handle customer feedback?

BusNGo encourages passengers to leave feedback and reviews for their travel experiences. You can view and respond to this feedback through the operator portal to improve your services.
